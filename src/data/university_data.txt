###Foreign Applicants
The credentials of an undergraduate applicant who holds a foreign nationality and who wishes to attend the University are evaluated in accordance with the general regulations governing admission of foreign nationals as approved by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Education, Government of Pakistan.
Candidates from foreign countries should send their applications through the Embassies of their countries in Islamabad, and these must be routed through the Ministries of Foreign Affairs and Education, Government of Pakistan.
No foreign student will be admitted in the University unless his/her admission after due scrutiny of his/her application, is confirmed by the University.

###About University
The EDR Trust and the NWFP Government Charted the Northern University in the private sector, in accordance with NWFP Ordinance No. XLVIII of 2002, which was published in Extraordinary Gazette No. P.111 on November 2, 2002. The Higher Education Commission(HEC) recognizes it as a private University that confers degrees. The foundation stone was laid by the Governor, KP, in April 2005, and the inauguration of the campus was done by General Sawar Khan, President Northern University on 14th November 2008. Northern University was upgraded to the "W" level (formerly the "A" category) in March 2009. The university has one main campus located on Wattar  Road, Nowshera, providing a convenient location for visitors from all across the country.

The University offers uniform and comprehensive programs that focus on subjects like Business Administration, Education, Computer Science, Basic Sciences, Social Sciences, Allied Health, Nursing, Humanities, and other related fields. These programs help students gain the necessary skills and knowledge to tackle the challenges of the new era. Currently, the University is comprised of four faculties, each equipped with sufficient resources to excel in their respective areas:

Faculty of Administrative Sciences (FAS)

Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences (FASS)

Faculty of Engineering & Information Technology (FEIT)

Faculty of Sciences (FS)

Each of these faculties has the potential to become a center of excellence in its own domain.

Northern University provides top-notch facilities and state-of-the-art laboratories to its students. These facilities have been carefully designed to provide an enriching and dynamic learning environment, enabling students to excel in their chosen fields.

Northern University boasts cutting-edge labs that are equipped with the latest technology and equipment.These labs serve as hubs of innovation and practical learning. Whether it's the Faculty of Engineering & Information Technology, where students have access to computer labs and specialized equipment, or the Faculty of Sciences, with its research facilities, students can engage in hands-on experimentation and exploration.

Our university takes great strides to provide not only high-quality education but also a nurturing environment for practical learning and personal growth. The state-of-the-art labs, well-equipped facilities, and advanced resources collectively contribute to an educational journey that prepares our students to meet the challenges of the future with confidence and competence.
